We want to make resources for research easy and accessible for everyone. We are not here to tell you whether this is easy or tough; we are not here to decide that, but we will decode that for you. It took us some work and effort to get confidence to able to teach you about biostatistics and original studies, and we plan to help you with that efficiently after learning through our mentors who have been very kind to teach us. We plan to help you with all kinds of research studies possible and help you answer your questions. With the craze getting out there that research is a requirement for your CV - for jobs, for residency, for recognition - our mentors' were kind to inform us it is quality that trumpets quantity, something often overlooked in the hustle and peer-pressure, and something we might've succumbed to.
